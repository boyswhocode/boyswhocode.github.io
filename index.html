About the Club

We focus on three pillars: learning (workshops & curriculum), building (projects & hackathons), and leadership (mentorship & outreach). We aim to create a welcoming, inclusive environment so members can try tech without fear of judgment.

Code of Conduct

Be respectful. Support each other. Share credit. Harassment is not tolerated. If something happens, contact the faculty sponsor immediately.
